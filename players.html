Meet our stars that make us feeling proud:

Goalkeepers

#1. Manuel Neuer

Manuel Peter Neuer, born on March 27, 1986, in Gelsenkirchen, West Germany, is a German goalkeeper who currently plays for FC Bayern Munich. Standing at 6 ft 4 in (193 cm), Neuer is widely regarded as one of the best goalkeepers in the world. Known for his exceptional shot-stopping ability, commanding presence, and distribution skills, he has redefined the role of a modern goalkeeper.

#26. Sven Ulreich

Sven Ulreich, born on August 3, 1988, in Schorndorf, Germany, is a German goalkeeper who has played for FC Bayern Munich. Standing at 192 cm, Ulreich has made significant contributions to the team during his time at the club. Ulreich joined FC Bayern Munich in 2015 and served as a reliable backup goalkeeper during his tenure. While he primarily played as the understudy to Manuel Neuer, Ulreich showcased his abilities whenever called upon. He demonstrated good shot-stopping skills, agility, and a strong presence between the posts.

#27. Yann Sommer

Yann Sommer was born on 17 December 1988 in Morges and plays for FC Bayern München. The 1.83m-tall goalkeeper was in the starting line-up in all previous 27 games of the current season. Of these, he won 14 games with his club. He was victorious over FC Schalke 04 while playing for FC Bayern München with a score of 6-0. He played as a goalie throughout this entire game.
