Welcome to the best club

A club with a huge history...

FC Bayern Munich was founded on February 27, 1900. It has since become one of the most successful football clubs in the world. The team has a rich history and a strong tradition of success both domestically and internationally.

A club with a huge stadium...

FC Bayern plays its home matches at the Allianz Arena, which is located in the Fröttmaning district of Munich. The stadium has a seating capacity of around 75,000 for domestic matches and can expand to accommodate over 70,000 more for international fixtures.
